Biology and History
Birds haven't really evolved with humans, only some are domesticated and others tamed. Most are suited to flying, developing wings and feathers but some, like penguins and chickens haven't. They have are many different ways of feeding and many different species

Chickens, ducks and some geese are used for eggs and food and some species of birds like parrots are kept for companionship.
